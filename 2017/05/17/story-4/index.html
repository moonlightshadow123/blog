This is the fourth part of the story.

After some confirmation, the two jaded vagabonds sat beside the fire.
“Could I know your name?” Asked Noah, “And why you are here?”
“Um… Have we met somewhere else? Cause you look familiar to me.”
“Well, I don’t think so… I woke up here and couldn’t remember what happened. Have we met before?”
“Never mind, maybe I just mistook you as someone else. I’m Josh, Josh Maxwell. I traveled here several days ago and got lost. Should have followed the crew closer…”
“Noah Bowman, sorry to hide in the bush and meet you that way.”
“It’s fine, I thought it was some barbarians from some cannibal tribes. It’s much better to be a normal human being.” Josh grabbed another Kebab from the makeshift wood grill, and handed to Noah. “Try this, I’ve hunted some rats today. “
Noah abhorred rats, even just seeing them, but for now, either eat it or die here to feed them.
“Not as bad as I thought”, Noah took a bite with a grimace.
“You have to live with what you have here, man”, Josh chewed the cartilage so hard that you could hear it from a distance, mixed with the cracking of firewood. No more talking for a long while.

“So… Josh, do you have a plan to get out of here.” Noah broke the silence in a tentative tone.
“No, not for now.” Josh sighed sightly, “I have explored some places in the east in previous days. But there’s no signs of human inhabitation.”
“Maybe you can try heading other directions.”
“By the way, dude, which direction did you came in?”
“Probably that way.” Noah pointed to the other side of the shallow lake. It’s to the north of where they were.
“You said that you don’t remember anything after you found yourself there, right?”
“Yeah, “ Noah nodded marginally, “I guess it’s because of my head injury. It is still aching.”
“Let me check it out.”
Josh took a close look at Noah’s head.
“It’s alright, probably a concussion, but you will be fine after some rest.”
“And there’re some bruises here on my arms and hip.” Noah rolled up the sleeves of his shredded shirt. Contusions were already darking and swelling.
“I bet you were beaten by someone, Noah.” Josh examined the bruises carefully.
“I totally agree. It can’t be done naturally.”

“That is to say you are beaten and abandoned here in the desert.”, Josh pondered for a while, “Here’s a crazy idea, dude. What if we follow back along the direction you came here, and find those people who abandoned you.”
“Um… I don’t know, man.” Noah clearly had some scruples about the plan, “They obviously wanted me to die. Going back is sort of like biting the hook.”
“OK, up to you, bro.” Josh shrugged, “But if we have searched all other directions, it could be our last straw.”
“Yeah, there may be bigger chance of encountering other people there, but we can explore a little bit other directions first.” Noah said.
“OK then, you do some rest tomorrow and I will go west. You can’t go far with so many injuries.”
“Thanks, bro. It will be great.”

As the preliminary plan sketched out, they went to sleep in the coarse haystack Josh made previously.
